Rules

By agreeing with network rules you agree with:

- you may not insult anyone
- you may not spread hate and lies
- you may not propagate racism, nazism, or any other content related against other ethnicities, religions and ideals
- you may not propagate cheating, hacking or any other means of gaining advantage over other players
- you may not distribute violent content or pornography
- behave nice and with respect to others
- admins of private servers can not use disrespectful or insulting server names or descriptions

Breaking any of these conditions can be punished by adequate treatment as forum moderators decide, thus it can be one of these:

- deleting or editing your post in order to make it suitable with these rules
- prohibited rights for writing posts at forum
- temporary IP ban from forum
- temporary IP ban from CryMP and all it's services including in-game services
- permanent IP ban from CryMP and all it's services including in-game services and account removal


These rules may change anytime, you can always find link to them at bottom of the page or in help section.
